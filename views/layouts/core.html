Historic gameplay brings Bulldog victory against Harvard

Once the protests had wrapped up at the November gridiron showdown against Harvard and gameplay resumed, Yale managed to come back from a 3619 deficit late in the fourth quarter and brought the game to a tie. In triple overtime, the Bulldogs completed their comeback, and one of the greatest games in the already storied rivalry between Harvard and Yale came to an epic finish with Yale winning 5043. Not only did this clash go down in history, but it also gave the Blue and White a piece of the Ivy League title. The Bulldogs finished the season tied with Dartmouth in first place, delivering Yale its second Ivy League championship in three years. (Photo: Daniel Zhao, photography editor.)
